Response format

All items were measured on a 7-point scale with Likert-response format. Answers ranged between:

  •  -3 completely disagree
  •  -2 disagree
  •  -1 slightly disagree
  •   0 neutral
  • +1 slightly agree
  • +2 agree
  • +3 strongly agree

All items were translated from German.
